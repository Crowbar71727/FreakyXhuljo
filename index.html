WISHLIST

Subnautica 2 is an underwater survival adventure game set on an all-new alien ocean world. It is the next chapter in the Subnautica universe, developed by Unknown Worlds.

Explore a vast, vibrant, and dangerous alien ocean. Craft tools, build habitats, and discover the mysteries of a hostile world. Survival depends on your wits and courage.

GAME FEATURES

EXPLORE ALIEN BIOMES

Dive into a vast, uncharted alien ocean. Discover new biomes, each with unique flora and fauna, hidden caves, and ancient secrets. Every dive offers a new adventure and breathtaking views.

GENETIC EVOLUTION

Adapt and evolve to survive the perilous depths. Utilize advanced genetic modification technology to enhance your abilities, resist harsh environments, and even bond with certain lifeforms to gain unique advantages.

RESOURCE GATHERING & CRAFTING

Scavenge for vital resources across the ocean floor. Craft essential tools, vehicles, and habitats to establish your base of operations and explore deeper into the unknown. Survival depends on your ingenuity.

Explorer's Codex: Item Cards

Click on cards for details:

Survival Knife

Getting Started: Essential for early game resource gathering and defense.

Basic Controls: Left click to swing, right click to hold.

Game Glossary: "Tool" - a handheld device for interaction.

Oxygen Tank

Getting Started: Craft early to extend dive times. Crucial for exploration.

Basic Controls: Automatically equips when in water.

Game Glossary: "Resource" - a material used for crafting.

Seamoth

Getting Started: Build in a Vehicle Bay for faster, safer exploration.

Basic Controls: WASD to move, Space to ascend, Ctrl to descend.

Game Glossary: "Vehicle" - a craftable machine for traversal.

Fins

Getting Started: Equip to increase swim speed significantly.

Basic Controls: Automatically enhances swim speed.

Game Glossary: "Gear" - equipable item providing passive bonuses.

Explorer's Codex: Advanced Tools

More tools for deeper exploration:

Seaglide

Getting Started: A handheld propulsion vehicle for fast underwater travel.

Basic Controls: Hold left click to activate, use WASD for direction.

Game Glossary: "Gadget" - a portable electronic device.

Laser Cutter

Getting Started: Used to cut open sealed doors and wreckage.

Basic Controls: Point and hold left click to cut.

Game Glossary: "Tool" - specialized equipment for specific tasks.

Propulsion Cannon

Getting Started: Grabs and propels objects, useful for clearing paths.

Basic Controls: Left click to grab, right click to propel.

Game Glossary: "Utility" - an item with practical applications.

Scanner Room

Getting Started: Base module for scanning surrounding biomes for resources and lifeforms.

Basic Controls: Interact with console to deploy scanner drones.

Game Glossary: "Module" - a component of a larger structure.
